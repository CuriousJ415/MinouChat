MegaCorp Inc. Business Plan
===============================

Executive Summary:
MegaCorp is a technology company focused on AI solutions for businesses and consumers. Our mission is to make advanced AI accessible to everyone while maintaining complete privacy and security.

Revenue Target: $5 million by 2025

Key Products:
1. AI Assistant Platform - Personal productivity tool
2. Document Processing System - Automated document analysis
3. Business Intelligence Dashboard - Data-driven insights

Market Analysis:
The AI market is growing rapidly at 25% annually. Our competitive advantage is privacy-first design and local processing capabilities.

Financial Projections:
Year 1: $1M revenue, 10 employees
Year 2: $3M revenue, 25 employees  
Year 3: $5M revenue, 40 employees

Technology Stack:
- Machine Learning: PyTorch, HuggingFace Transformers
- Backend: FastAPI, Python
- Frontend: React, TypeScript
- Database: PostgreSQL, Vector databases
- Infrastructure: Docker, Kubernetes
