Company Policies and Guidelines
===============================

Remote Work Policy:
- All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Mandatory office days are Tuesday and Thursday
- Core hours: 9 AM - 3 PM in your local timezone
- Monthly all-hands meetings required on-site

Vacation Policy:
- Employees receive 20 days of paid vacation annually
- Additional 5 personal days for emergencies
- Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance
- No blackout periods except during product launches

Benefits:
- Health insurance: 100% coverage for employee, 80% for family
- Retirement: 6% 401k match
- Professional development: $2,000 annual budget
- Equipment: Laptop, monitor, ergonomic setup provided
- Wellness: Gym membership reimbursement up to $100/month

Performance Reviews:
- Quarterly check-ins with manager
- Annual performance review with 360-degree feedback
- Promotion cycles in January and July
- Merit increases based on performance and market rates

Code of Conduct:
- Respect and professionalism required at all times
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Confidentiality agreements apply to all proprietary information
- Social media guidelines: No sharing of internal company information
